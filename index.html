The Why

Like most people, I dont want to be stuck at a job i don't like. As they say do a job that you love and you never have to work a day in your life. Sounds easy if you if you know what you love and that love bears fruit.
Now i dont know what i love. At some point i knew what i wanted to do but as i was pursuing on that journey i realised, that i dont truly love it but i just liked to do it. If i kept on going on that journey, i didn't see myself happy or fullfilled at the end of that tunel. "If i'm going to try go all the way" -Charles Bukowski. Now I had to find something else that can let me have free time to do my other projets, while still doing what i like.

Career objective:

  • Flexible time
  • Flexible enviroment/Freelancer
  • Secure/abemdant job ( Has a future )
  • Be creative
Nothing else fits better than a programmer. Specifically Full stack developper. If its not full stack than i can jump to data analysist or so on. It can help me greatly on my other projects as well. And what i found surprisingly is that it also helps me understand the mind . Its sounds too good to be true.
